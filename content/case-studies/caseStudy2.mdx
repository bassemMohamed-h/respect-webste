## Project Overview

PlayZone is a smart mobile application designed for booking football fields and organizing matches quickly and reliably.
We focused on reducing friction in booking and helping players find nearby games.

## Our Role

- Market positioning
- Audience definition
- Core messaging
- Go-to-market direction
- Communication strategy

## Research & Insights

### Key Insights

- Players want instant booking confirmations and clear availability.
- Teams care more about reliability than discounts.
- Trust signals (reviews, verified venues) increase conversion.

### Research Focus

- Existing booking behavior and pain points
- Competitor feature comparison
- Decision factors for venue selection

## Strategy Direction

We positioned PlayZone as the fastest way to book and manage football matches, built around clarity and trust.

- Lead with “book in seconds” messaging
- Improve venue profiles with strong proof (photos, ratings, facilities)
- Make the match flow social (invite, split cost, reminders)

We then designed the UI system to support quick scanning, confident booking, and smooth repeat usage.
